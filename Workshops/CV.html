Workshop - Computer Vision

Op deze pagina zijn de opdrachten te vinden welke passend zijn bij de lesstof welke tijdens de workshop Computer Vision zijn behandeld

Assignments

Portfolio Assignment 3. Add Color

Add a colour to the sketch you made in the Getting Started Assignment. Make a screenshot or recording of the result of your sketch and add this to your portfolio.

Portfolio Assignment 7. Computer Vision

Experiment/tinker with some of the examples linked above. Try to change some of the code to make it do something else. Make a screenshot or recording of the result of your sketch and add this to your portfolio.

Portfolio Assignment 8. Hand position and distance

Experiment with these wrist keypoints and/or calculating the distance between wrist keypoints. Visualize this in an interesting way in the sketch. For example, draw a box on the screen that fits perfectly between your hands. Or detect when someone is extending their arms like flying an airplane.

Portfolio Assignment 9. Cheer Detection

After familiarizing yourself with detecting the keypoints, here is an exercise with a very specific goal. Make a sketch that detect if someone is cheering by analysing the position of their hands. When cheering is detected visualise that in an interesting way.
